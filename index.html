How It Works

A reporting workflow, not a generic AI box

Quickler is being built to handle one repeated reporting loop properly.

That means capturing what happened on site, turning it into a structured draft, and keeping review in control before anything is sent. The point is not to replace judgment. It is to remove the waste between capture and finished report.

Capture on site

  • Voice, photos, and text in a familiar chat workflow.
  • No heavy app adoption burden on engineers or technicians.
  • Natural capture during a live inspection, survey, or visit.

Draft in structure

  • Turns raw capture into a near-finished report shape.
  • Fits repeated technical report structures.
  • Reduces copy-paste and rewrite time.

Review before send

  • Human review stays in the loop before final send.
  • Corrections can be made in plain language.
  • Trust, evidence, and sign-off stay intact.
