About

Quickler was founded by Philip Ross, an automation consultant based in Dundee, Scotland. Philip studied Electrical and Mechanical Engineering at the University of Strathclyde and later worked at Dijuno, an AI analytics startup, where he built experience in LLM testing, prompt engineering, and Python automation.

His work combines practical workflow software with a broader interest in building useful systems from the ground up. Across client projects and independent builds, that includes automation tools, internal platforms, product prototypes, data-heavy applications, local-first software, and personal training technology such as his AI cycling coach.

The common thread is the same: thoughtful systems, real-world usefulness, and software designed to help people work more clearly.
