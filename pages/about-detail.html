About

Quickler Ltd was founded by Philip Ross. Philip studied Electrical and Mechanical Engineering at the University of Strathclyde and later worked at Dijuno, an AI analytics startup, where he built experience in LLM testing, prompt engineering, and Python automation.

His work is now focused on one product problem: helping technical and field-based teams turn notes, photos, and captured job information into finished reports faster.

The common thread is the same: thoughtful systems, real-world usefulness, and software designed to reduce admin without losing trust or structure.
