Quickler Site Reports

Software for teams still writing the report after the visit.

Quickler Site Reports is for field, inspection, survey, maintenance, compliance, and operations teams whose staff gather the facts during the job, then still spend hours turning them into a formal document afterwards.

Best fit

Field, inspection, survey, maintenance, compliance, and operations teams

A good fit where the work happens first, the report follows a recognisable shape, and the writing-up still happens later and takes far too long.

Core workflow

Capture once, write once

The aim is simple: capture the work properly on site and cut down the time spent rewriting it at a desk.

Current stage

Focused early pilot

This is a narrow early pilot, not a grand transformation programme. It starts with one repeated visit record, report, or paperwork flow that already matters to the business.

What it is for

This is for businesses where the facts are gathered during the job, but the proper report, record, or compliance write-up is still assembled afterwards from notes, photos, voice notes, measurements, or memory.

What it should do

  • Let staff record the job properly while they are on site
  • Shape that material into the right report format or workflow template
  • Keep final review with the person responsible for the report
  • Save time without making the process feel less reliable

What makes a good pilot

The best starting point is one repeated report or site document, enough volume for the pain to be worth fixing, and real examples of the current paperwork.

Good fit

Repeated reporting with a stable shape

Inspection reports, survey summaries, maintenance visit reports, site investigation records, QA packs, and compliance paperwork with a clear repeated shape.

Not ideal

No stable report pattern

If every report is materially different, or if review and evidence are barely part of the job, this is probably not the right first fit.
