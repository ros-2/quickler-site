Product Direction

Quickler is becoming a chat-first reporting product.

The main direction now is Quickler Reports: a product for technical teams who capture notes, photos, and voice on site and do not want to rebuild the report later from scratch. Consulting still exists where it helps validate or shape the workflow.

01 · Core workflow

Capture on site, not twice

The core problem is technical reporting that gets captured once in rough form and then rewritten later into a formal document. Quickler aims to close that gap.

02 · Product shape

Chat-first reporting

The intended front end is simple capture through familiar channels, with structured report generation and workflow logic underneath.

03 · Current pilots

Early validation with real teams

Right now the focus is on learning from real reporting workflows, validating the first niche, and proving the capture-to-draft loop in practice.

04 · Where consulting fits

Consulting as a bridge, not the end state

Consulting still matters when it helps understand the workflow, shape a pilot, or solve a nearby operational problem. But the direction of travel is product, not agency sprawl.
