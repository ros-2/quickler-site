Product Direction

Quickler is becoming a chat-first reporting product.

Quickler Reports is for teams who should be applying for an early pilot now: inspections, surveys, site visits, compliance work, and maintenance teams that still rebuild reports later from rough field capture.

01 · Core workflow

Capture on site, not twice

The core problem is technical reporting that gets captured once in rough form and then rewritten later into a formal document. Quickler aims to close that gap.

02 · Product shape

Chat-first reporting

The intended front end is simple capture through familiar channels, with structured report generation and workflow logic underneath.

03 · Current pilots

Early validation with real teams

Right now the focus is on getting the right early teams into pilots, validating the first niche, and proving the capture-to-draft loop in practice.

04 · Product aim

Get the report done with the hard hat still on

The point is not generic automation. The point is to help teams finish repeated reports much earlier, with less admin, less retyping, and less information lost between site visit and final output.

Want to join an early pilot?

Apply if your team has one repeated report workflow that should be much faster and much lighter to run.
