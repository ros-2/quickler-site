How It Works

Four steps from site to finished document.

1. Capture

Capture on site

Staff send text, voice notes, photos, and measurements to a workflow number while they are on site, not after they get back to the desk.

2. Extract

Sort the facts into the right structure

quickler reads the messages, recognises the workflow you configured, and slots the content into the correct report, record, or template structure.

3. Review

The engineer stays in charge

The responsible person checks the draft, edits anything they want, and signs off. Nothing ships without a human eye.

4. Output

Finished report or admin pack

Produce the PDF, the document, or the next workflow state. Store the evidence alongside it so the record stands up later.
