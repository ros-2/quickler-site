Workflow Fit

What the product should do in practice.

What it is for

This is for businesses where the facts are gathered during the job, but the proper report, record, or compliance write-up is still assembled afterwards from notes, photos, voice notes, measurements, or memory.

What it should do

  • Let staff record the job properly while they are on site, through a simple WhatsApp-style workflow
  • Shape that material into the right report format or workflow template
  • Keep final review with the person responsible for the report
  • Save time without making the process feel vague, risky, or less reliable

What makes a good pilot

The best starting point is one repeated report or site document, enough volume for the pain to be worth fixing, and real examples of the current paperwork. That gives quickler enough structure to improve the workflow without asking your team to gamble on something vague.
