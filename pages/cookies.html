Cookie Policy

This page explains how quickler.co uses cookies and local browser storage. The site is set up to avoid loading analytics unless you actively allow them.

What Quickler uses now

Quickler currently uses a small browser-stored preference to remember whether you accepted or declined analytics cookies. If you decline, analytics scripts are not loaded.

Strictly necessary storage

The cookie consent tool stores your preference so the site can remember your choice. This is used only to operate the consent mechanism itself.

Analytics cookies

If you accept analytics, Quickler uses Google Analytics to measure general website usage such as page views, referral sources, device/browser information, and rough location patterns. This helps improve the site and understand which pages are useful.

Third-party services

Analytics and consent functions may rely on third-party services such as Google Analytics and the cookie-consent tooling used on the site. Those providers may set or read their own cookies in line with their service design.

Managing your choice

You can change your analytics choice at any time by using the cookie settings link in the footer. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated if the site's analytics, consent, or tracking setup changes.
