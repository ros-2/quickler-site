Scripture verification system

A review-focused tool for Christian publishers who need accurate, auditable checking of Scripture references and quotations.

The problem

Editorial teams still spend large amounts of time manually checking whether references exist, whether quotations match the stated translation, and whether deviations are acceptable or accidental.

The work is essential, but it is slow, repetitive, and difficult to audit after the fact.

What the system does

The system processes a manuscript and produces a separate verification document designed for editorial review.

  • Confirms that references exist
  • Compares quoted text against the ESV word-for-word
  • Detects likely use of other translations
  • Flags deviations with clear review signals
  • Produces side-by-side comparisons for editors

What it is and is not

  • An editorial verification tool for Christian publishers
  • Designed for accuracy, traceability, and review
  • Not a theological decision engine
  • Not a replacement for human editors

Who it is for

  • Christian publishing houses
  • Theological editors and proof-readers
  • Publishers handling mixed-translation manuscripts

Development status

The product is currently in development planning, with architecture and workflows defined and an initial customer use-case established.
