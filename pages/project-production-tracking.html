Production tracking streamlining

Publishing house

Date Nov 2025 Build time 4 hours Saves 14 days/year

Book status information was scattered across five spreadsheets. Checking whether a book would meet its deadline meant searching multiple files and chasing several people for updates.

Quickler built one tracker showing each book’s stage, owner, and deadline, with clear visual signals for items at risk.

Two hours per week of status chasing disappeared. The result was not just speed, but much clearer publishing operations.
