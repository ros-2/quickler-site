PO workflow platform

Quantum computing startup operations

Date Feb 2026 Build time 36 hours Outcome End-to-end PO workflow

Quickler built a custom purchase order platform for a quantum computing startup, with guided form entry, saved vendors and addresses, and line-item memory for faster repeat orders.

Approval routing by email, approver signature handling, branded PDF generation, and status tracking were added so the workflow could move from pending to approved or denied without disappearing into inboxes.

Paperless indexing links, history cloning, and setup automation were also integrated so new environments could start quickly with minimal manual steps.
