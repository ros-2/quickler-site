Till data automation

Multi-million pound charitable enterprise

Date Dec 2025 Build time 7 hours Saves 11.5 days/year

Two staff members were spending time every week pulling till data, reformatting it, and loading it into the main tracking spreadsheet by hand.

quickler added automation in Google Sheets to process the raw exports and feed the main spreadsheet automatically. Northern Ireland became fully automatic and the Scotland workflow dropped to around twenty minutes weekly.

The result was 100 minutes saved per week, or around 11.5 working days recovered each year.
