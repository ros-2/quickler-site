Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects do you take on?

Repetitive data work, manual processes, disconnected systems. Recent projects: retail data consolidation, grant writing automation, production tracking, business planning research.

How long does a typical project take?

2-3 days for single-process automation, 2-4 weeks for complex integrations. Timeline confirmed after scoping call.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

Primarily remote. Based in Scotland, available Scotland-wide and UK travel (costs quoted separately).

What happens after delivery?

You receive documentation, training, and own all code/systems built. Optional ongoing support available.

Do you work with charities?

Yes - discounted project pricing available for registered charities and non-profits. Contact me to discuss your needs.

What's your pricing?

Fixed project-based pricing agreed upfront. After initial contact and problem identification phase, you'll receive a clear quote with no surprises. See the Services & Process page for details on how pricing works.

Still have questions?

Contact us and I'll answer all your questions about automation, pricing, and how it could work for your organisation.
